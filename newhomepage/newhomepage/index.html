What is BrightCOIN

We are a technology platform that enables entrepreneurs to create a Securities Token Offerings (STO). You can create a token, setup your smart contracts, and raise funds in a legally compliant manner. A basic DIY STO can be launched with zero upfront fees with no coding required.

BrightCOIN can create asset-backed and non asset-backed Security Tokens. These tokens can represent equity, debt, revenue share, profit participation and much more.

How it Works

Step 1

Create a token & setup your offering

Create your token using our simple token creation wizard. You can customize all elements of your token including name, symbol and price. Next, you'll setup your token for your private placement. You can set the start/end dates, bonuses, rewards, caps and more.

Step 2

Launch your token offering

Once you setup your token offering we'll give you all the links required to launch. Links include signup and login pages for your investors. You simply hyperlink our urls to your landing page and you're ready to start collecting funds!

Step 3

Issue and distribute your tokens

With the simple click of a button you can distribute your tokens to contributors, advisors, founders, employees, etc. You can also list your tokens on our partner exchange and get liquidity immediately following your token offering (coming soon).

TOTAL TRANSPARENCY

You get access to a real-time backend that displays your token sale progress including advance analytics on investors, funds deposited and more.

Regulatory Compliance

Our “Programable securities” enable compliance not only during your token offering, but also during secondary exchange trading. You can set BrightCoin smart contracts to adhere to all rules and regulations.

KYC Aware

Ensure compliance by allowing transfer of your tokens only to investors that are KYC/AML verified. EG: Only KYC whitelisted wallets can receive tokens.

Accreditation Aware

Enable or disable transfer of tokens to investors based on their accreditation status. Eg: Only Accredited to Accredited transfers allowed.

Jurisdiction Aware

Enable or disable transfer of tokens to investors based on their jurisdiction. Eg: Non U.S to non U.S investor transfer allowed.

Investors Count Aware

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a coder to create my token and STO?

No expensive solidity coders required! BrightCOIN enables you to setup a coin and launch your STO without writing a single line of code. Follow the guidelines on our simple coin-creator form and you'll have your coin ready in minutes.

How does BrightCOIN safeguard against hackers?

One of our highest priorities is security. We go to great lengths to ensure that our smart contracts are as secure as humanly possible. Our application has undergone penetration testing and smart contract are audited giving you peace of mind.

Which cryptocurrencies are accepted?

It's a well know fact that enabling more cryptocurrencies increases your funding amount. With BrightCOIN your investors can add funds using all major cryptocurrencies including BTC, BCH, ETH, LTC, XLM, EOS, DASH and DOGE.

Can I display crowdsale progress on my site?

Yes, BrightCOIN gives you an API that'll enable you to display crowdsale progress, amount funded, coin calculator, token balance and more on your website.

Can I set up bonuses and rewards?

Yes, BrightCOIN enables deep customization of your STO. You can set up bonuses based on start and end dates, maximum and minimum amount per period and rewards based on referrals.

Can I see how much money I have raised?

Yes, you get access to our live "STO COCKPIT" that gives you full transparency and access to all important data points for your STO. You can see number of signup, total amount raised, average funding amount and more.
